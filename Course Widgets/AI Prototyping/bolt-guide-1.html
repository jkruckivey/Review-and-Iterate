Before You Begin

This guide will walk you through building your first app using Bolt.new. Bolt is an AI-powered platform that generates complete applications from your descriptions - including frontend, backend, and database. Everything runs in your browser with no installation needed. Here's what you'll need:

  • A modern web browser (Chrome or Firefox)
  • An idea for a simple app you want to build
  • About 25-35 minutes of your time
