Apply your knowledge of precision and recall to a real-world fraud detection scenario

The Scenario

Your company has deployed a machine learning model to detect fraudulent transactions. After running for one month, the fraud detection team has compiled the results and needs your help analyzing the model's performance.

Here's what happened: The model flagged 150 transactions as potentially fraudulent. When the fraud team investigated these flagged transactions:

Total transactions flagged by model: 150
Actually fraudulent (correctly flagged): 80
Legitimate (incorrectly flagged): 70

Additionally, the fraud team's manual review found that 20 fraudulent transactions were NOT caught by the model and slipped through undetected.

Calculate the Metrics

Hint: Precision = (True Positives) / (True Positives + False Positives)

Hint: Recall = (True Positives) / (True Positives + False Negatives)

Which Metric Should You Optimize?

Given this fraud detection context, which metric would you prioritize optimizing, and why?
