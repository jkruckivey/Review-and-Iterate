Challenge 1: Revenue by Category × Region

Drag Product Category to Rows, Shipping Region to Columns, and Total Revenue to Values.

Success criteria: Electronics revenue should be approximately $1,424,000

Step-by-step:
  1. Find "📦 Product Category" in the field list and drag it to the "Rows" drop zone
  2. Find "🌍 Shipping Region" and drag it to the "Columns" drop zone
  3. Find "💰 Total Revenue" and drag it to the "∑ Values" drop zone
  4. Check that Electronics row shows approximately $1,424,000 total
Challenge 2: Monthly Trend Analysis

Create a monthly revenue trend and visualize it with a line chart.

  1. Drag Order Date to Rows → Right-click → Group by Month
  2. Drag Total Revenue to Values
  3. Click "Insert PivotChart" → Select Line Chart

Success criteria: December revenue should be approximately $530,000

Tip: After dragging Order Date to Rows, right-click on it to see grouping options. Choose "Group by Month" to see monthly aggregations instead of individual dates.
Challenge 3: Product Analysis

Analyze products by revenue and transaction count.

  1. Drag Product Name to Rows
  2. Drag Total Revenue to Values
  3. Drag Order Date to Values (2nd time) → Right-click → Change to Count

Success criteria: Wireless Headphones should show approximately $190,000

Tip: You can drag the same field to Values multiple times with different aggregations. Right-click the second instance to change from Sum to Count.
