Expense Classification Activity

How to Use This Activity

Objective: Drag each expense item to the correct expense category based on the definitions provided in the reference guide.

Instructions:

  • Review the Expense Categories Reference Guide below
  • Drag expense items from the left panel to the appropriate category boxes on the right
  • Receive instant feedback on your classifications
  • Use the "Show/Hide Reference Guide" button if you need help
  • Try to classify all expenses correctly to complete the activity

Expense Categories Reference Guide

COGS

Description: Direct costs of producing goods sold

Examples: Raw materials, manufacturing labor, factory overhead

Operating Expenses

Description: Costs of running the business

Examples: Salaries, rent, utilities, marketing

Interest Expense

Description: Cost of borrowing money

Examples: Interest on loans, bonds

Income Tax Expense

Description: Taxes owed on profits

Examples: Corporate income taxes
