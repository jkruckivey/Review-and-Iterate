Key Decision Factors

Internet Access - Highest Efficiency
With an 87.5% CM ratio, internet access has the lowest variable costs relative to revenue. However, it requires capital investment in computers and may have limited capacity.
Food Items - Highest Dollar Contribution
Food generates $4.80 per unit in contribution margin (the highest absolute amount), but requires more labor, inventory management, and has a lower CM ratio at 60%.
Coffee & Beverages - Traffic Driver
Coffee has high volume potential and brings customer traffic that can lead to cross-selling opportunities. The 70% CM ratio is strong, and coffee requires minimal labor compared to food.
Recommended Strategy
A balanced mix approach: Leverage coffee for customer traffic and volume, food for higher absolute margins, and internet access for incremental revenue with minimal variable costs. This diversified strategy reduces risk and maximizes overall profitability.
