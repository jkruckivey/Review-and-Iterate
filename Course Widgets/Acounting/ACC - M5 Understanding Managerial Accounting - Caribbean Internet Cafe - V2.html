Key Learning Insights

Understanding Contribution Margin

Contribution Margin (CM) = Price - Variable Cost per Unit. This tells you how much each sale contributes toward covering fixed costs and generating profit.

CM Ratio = (Contribution Margin ÷ Price) × 100%. This shows the percentage of each sales dollar that contributes to fixed costs and profit.

Strategic Decision Making

  • Highest CM Ratio: Internet access (87.5%) - Most efficient per dollar of sales
  • Highest Absolute CM: Food items ($4.80) - Most profit per unit sold
  • Volume Potential: Coffee - High traffic driver with good 70% CM ratio

Recommended Strategy: A balanced product mix leveraging coffee for customer traffic, food for higher dollar margins, and internet for incremental revenue with minimal variable costs.
