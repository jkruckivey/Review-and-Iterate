The Hits
~10%
A handful of titles generate returns large enough to fund an entire studio. The upside is extraordinary — but access to it is rare.
The Turkeys
~90%
The vast majority of titles fail to recoup their production costs. High costs are certain. Profits are not. Most bets lose.
The implication

To survive in a creative market, you need resources enough to place a portfolio of bets. A single bet — or a small number of bets — is most likely to leave you a loser, regardless of how confident you are in your ability to pick winners.
