Three Approaches to Ambidexterity

How organizations balance exploration and exploitation

Approach 1
Structural Ambidexterity
🔭 Exploration
⚙️ Exploitation
Separate exploration and exploitation into distinct units, each with its own culture, leadership, and ways of working.
Example: bank innovation labs
Approach 2
Sequential Ambidexterity
🔭 Exploration
→
⚙️ Exploitation
→
Can firms cycle back?
orientation shifts over time
Organizations alternate between exploration and exploitation over time -- typically starting exploration-oriented and shifting toward exploitation as they mature and scale.
Pattern: young firm to mature firm
Approach 3
Contextual Ambidexterity
🔭 Innovation hat
⚙️ Efficiency hat
same organization, same people, shifting modes
Keep both modes alive within the same organization, asking individuals and teams to shift between exploration and exploitation depending on what the moment requires.
Same team, different modes
