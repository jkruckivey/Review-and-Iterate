One Design Wins
The market commits. Competitors and future innovators must conform.
The Consequence
The winning design sets the template for all future innovation in that space. The battle is over. The standard is set.
Key Idea
Technical superiority alone does not determine which design wins a standards battle. Market power, strategic alliances, pricing, and timing all play a role — and the winner shapes the future of an entire product category.
