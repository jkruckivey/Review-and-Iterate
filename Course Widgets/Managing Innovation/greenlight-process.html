The problem

This all looks logical and reasonable. But every phase of the process relies on the same flawed assumption: that reviewers can identify which projects are likely to succeed based on comparisons to what has worked before. As MechWarrior 2 showed, the process is as likely to kill a future hit as to protect against a future turkey.
