The Aftermath: Bavarian Nordic

What actually happened to ABNCoV2

February 2023
Delay Announced
Bavarian Nordic announces a delay to its Phase 3 clinical trial. Recruiting subjects is proving harder than expected.
June 2023
Trial Results
ABNCoV2 matches mRNA vaccines against the original Wuhan strain. But against newer mutations such as Omicron XBB.1.5, it produces considerably lower neutralizing antibody levels. Like mRNA vaccines, it would need annual updates. Unlike them, the technology cannot be adapted quickly enough to meet seasonal timelines.
August 2023
Program Ends
Bavarian Nordic announces that ABNCoV2 no longer represents a commercial opportunity for the company. The program is discontinued.
