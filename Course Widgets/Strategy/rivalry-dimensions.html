How market conditions and competition basis affect competitive intensity

Growth + Differentiation

New customers available. Companies compete on unique value, not price. Room for multiple winners.

Low Rivalry

Growth + Price Competition

Growing market absorbs some pressure, but price focus erodes margins.

Decline + Differentiation

Shrinking pie, but differentiation protects margins. Winners take share from exits.

Medium Rivalry

Worst case: shrinking market + commoditized products = price wars and margin destruction.
