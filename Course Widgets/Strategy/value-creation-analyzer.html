The "Better Before Cheaper" Pattern

Across thousands of companies studied, those creating value through non-price differentiation consistently outperform those competing primarily on cost. 7 of 9 Miracle Workers compete at the high-end; 7 of 9 Average Joes compete on price. Even in "price" industries like discount retail, winners like Family Dollar differentiate on access, convenience, and basket size—not just cost.
