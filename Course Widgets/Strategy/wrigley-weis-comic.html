The Moral Revenue Before Cost
THE SECOND RULE

Revenue Before Cost

Cost-based advantages are particularly vulnerable because:

👁️ Costs are easier to see and replicate than differentiation
📈 Scale benefits larger players disproportionately
🏃 There's no loyalty to "cheapest"—customers switch instantly
Your Turn The Takeaway
This story doesn't say cost-driven strategies CAN'T work.

The Real Lesson

"On average, across thousands of companies over decades, revenue-driven strategies are more likely to produce exceptional, sustained results."

When analyzing any company, ask:
"Is their advantage based on what customers pay... or what the company spends?"
