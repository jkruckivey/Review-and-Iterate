The "Revenue Before Cost" Pattern

Research on thousands of companies shows that 84% of the advantage between Miracle Workers and Long Runners comes from gross margin—revenue advantages. More than 40% of Miracle Workers actually have HIGHER costs than Long Runners but still outperform through pricing power. While cost advantages can work, revenue-based profitability is more durable and more often associated with exceptional performance.
