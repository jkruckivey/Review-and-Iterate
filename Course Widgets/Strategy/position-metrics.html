Strategic Position Metrics

Each position has distinct frequency, margins, and risk profiles

High-End

Premium performance, premium price

Market Frequency 20-25%
Gross Margins 25-40%
Exit Rate 8-12%
Primary Risk: Commodification

Mid-Market

Balanced performance and cost

Market Frequency 50-60%
Gross Margins 15-25%
Exit Rate 15-20%
Primary Risk: Strategic Drift

Price-Sensitive

Maximum efficiency, lowest cost

Market Frequency 15-20%
Gross Margins 8-15%
Exit Rate 10-15%
Primary Risk: Nowhere to Hide
