The Persistence vs. Change Framework

Heartland Express changed everything about its business model—assets, costs, processes—while preserving its strategic position (premium-priced reliable service). Maytag responded to every competitive pressure but gradually abandoned what made them successful. The key question isn't "should we change?" but "what should we preserve?" Change operations freely; preserve strategic position carefully. When even the strategy must change, recognize you're at its limits and need different thinking—potentially innovation, not just strategic adjustment.
