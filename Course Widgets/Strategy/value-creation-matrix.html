The Pattern Behind the Rules

Companies that follow both rules—competing on non-price value AND capturing that value through revenue rather than cost savings—consistently achieve Miracle Worker performance. Those competing on price while focused on cost reduction face the most crowded strategic space with highest failure rates. The "one rule" quadrants (Long Runners) can sustain solid performance but rarely achieve exceptional results.
