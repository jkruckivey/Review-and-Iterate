Key Insight: Current Competitive Landscape

The EV industry has largely reached parity on range (functionality). The current battleground is reliability—battery longevity, build quality, and software stability. Tesla's panel gaps, legacy automakers' software issues, and battery degradation concerns are all reliability battles. Convenience (charging network) is emerging as the next frontier as range anxiety decreases.

Key Insight: The Ecosystem Battleground

Smartphones have reached parity on functionality (camera, speed, display) and reliability. Apple's competitive advantage now rests heavily on convenience—the seamless integration across iPhone, iPad, Mac, Watch, and AirPods. This ecosystem lock-in explains why Apple can charge premium prices despite similar hardware specs. Price competition is emerging as Chinese manufacturers offer near-flagship specs at mid-range prices.

Key Insight: The Discovery Challenge

Streaming services have reached parity on functionality (4K, similar UI) and reliability (all work well). The current battleground is convenience—specifically content discovery and curation. Netflix invests heavily in recommendation algorithms; Disney+ bundles content logically. Price is emerging as the next major battleground with ad-supported tiers, password crackdowns, and bundle strategies indicating increased price competition.
