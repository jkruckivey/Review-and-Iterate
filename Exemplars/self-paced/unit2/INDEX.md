## Design Standards Applied Throughout

### Pedagogical Framework
- **Active Learning:** 60-70% of time spent in interactive activities
- **Bounded Choices:** Widgets use 5-10 options, not infinite possibilities
- **Instant Feedback:** All assessments provide non-judgmental, explanatory feedback
- **Formative Assessment:** Low-stakes checks throughout; no high-stakes exams until Unit 10
- **Real-World Case Study:** Canary Medical appears in all modules as running example

### Accessibility Standards
- **WCAG 2.2 AA Compliance:** All elements meet or exceed standards
- **Video Captions:** VTT format, 99.9% accuracy required
- **Keyboard Navigation:** All widgets fully operable without mouse
- **Screen Reader Support:** ARIA labels on all interactive elements
- **High Contrast:** 4.5:1 minimum on all text
- **Focus Indicators:** Visible 3px outline on all interactive elements
- **Responsive Design:** Works on 6" mobile through 27" desktop

### Design System Alignment
- **Font:** Geist (Google Fonts) throughout
- **Color Palette:** Ivey brand colors (#c5b783 accent, #2c3e50 slate)
- **Spacing:** Consistent 8px grid
- **Interactive Elements:** 1px borders, minimal aesthetic, no gradients/shadows
- **Typography:** Clear hierarchy, readable line heights (1.5-1.8)
