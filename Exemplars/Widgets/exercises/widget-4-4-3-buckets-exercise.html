Based on the Avatar behind-the-scenes footage you just watched, capture your observations in each of the three buckets below. Think about how the nature of work changed compared to the traditional moviemaking system described earlier in this session. There are no wrong answers -- this is about developing your observational lens. You will see the SME's detailed analysis in the next two modules.

Challenge
